# Developmental milestones
## Gross motor milestones
### In ventral suspension

|   Age    | Description                                                          |
| :------: | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1 month  | Head is below the plane of the rest of body due to *no neck control* |
| 2 months | Head is in the plane of the body. Neck control starts                |
| 3 months | Head goes above plane of body                                        |
### In prone position

|   Age    | Description                                                        |
| :------: | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2 weeks  | Baby lies on bed with high pelvis and knee drawn under the abdomen |
| 4 weeks  | Lifts chin of the bed momentarily                                  |
| 6 weeks  | Lies on bed with flat pelvis and extended hips                     |
| 8 weeks  | Lift face up at 45<sup>o</sup>                                     |
| 12 weeks | Can bear his weight on forearm with shoulder lifted off the couch  |
| 6 months | Can support his weight on hands or extended arms                   |

## Fine Motor

|     Age     | Description                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| :---------: | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
|   1 month   | Hands kept closed                                                                                                                                                                                |
|  2 months   | Hands opened intermittently                                                                                                                                                                      |
|  3 months   | <ul><li> Hands kept open <li> Hold an object when placed in hand <li> *Hand regard* </ul>                                                                                                        |
|  4 months   | Tries to reach an object but *overshoots*                                                                                                                                                        |
|  5 months   | *Bidextrous* grasp                                                                                                                                                                               |
|  6 months   | <ul><li> Unidextrous or palmar grasp <li> Can take biscuit to his mouth</ul>                                                                                                                     |
|  7 months   | Transfers object from one hand to other                                                                                                                                                          |
|  9 months   | *Immature*/assisted *pincer grasp*                                                                                                                                                               |
|  12 months  | <ul><li>*Mature*/unassited *pincer grasp*<li>Pulls off cap/mittens/socks</ul>                                                                                                                    |
|  15 months  | <ul><li>Scribbles spontatneously <li> Feeds self with cup <li> Tower of 2 cubes</ul>                                                                                                             |
|  18 months  | <ul><li>Tower of 3 cubes <li>Feeds self with spoon <li>Turns 2-3 pages at a time <li> unzips</ul>                                                                                                |
|   2 years   | <ul><li> Tower of 6-7 cubes <li> Can make a train with blocks <li> Turns doorknob or unscrews lid <li> Turns pages singly <li> Wears socks and shoes <li> Copies a horizontal vertical line</ul> |
|  2.5 years  | Makes a train with chiney                                                                                                                                                                        |
|   3 years   | <ul><li> Handedness gets established <li> Tower of 9-10 cubes <li> Copies a circle <li> Can dres/undress self except buttons</ul>                                                                |
|   4 years   | <ul><li> Copies a rectangle or a plus sign <li> Makes a bridge with cubes <li> Can button and unbutton <li> Catches ball reliably</ul>                                                           |
|   5 years   | <ul><li> Copies a traingle or multiplication sign or tilted cross <li> Can tie shoelaces <li> Makes a gate with cubes</ul>                                                                       |
| 6 - 7 years | <ul><li> Copies a diamond <li> Can make steps with cubes</ul>                                                                                                                                    |

## Vision milestones

|   Age    | Description                                                         |
| :------: | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
|  Birth   | Can fixate on a red dangling ring and follows it to 45<sup>o</sup>  |
| 4 weeks  | Can follow the red dangling ring to 90<sup>o</sup>                  |
| 12 weeks | Can follow the red dangling ring to 180<sup>o</sup>                 |
| 3 months | Fixates instantaneosly on an object shown to him (grasps with eyes) |
| 6 weeks  | Beginning of binocular vision                                       |
| 4 months | Establishment of binocular vision                                   |
|  1 year  | Follows rapidly moving objects                                      |
## Hearing

|    Age     | Description                                                                                    |
| :--------: | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
|  Neonate   | Respond to sound by startle/blinking/crying                                                    |
| 3-4 months | Turns head towards source of sound                                                             |
| 5-6 months | Turns head towards he source of sound then downwards if the source of sound is below ear level |
| 7-8 months | Localizes sound produced above ear level                                                       |
| 10 months  | Looks directly towards the source diagonally                                                   |
